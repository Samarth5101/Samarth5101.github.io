Heart Attack Prediction System

Heart Attack Predicition System is a heart attack prediction website developed by me and my teammates at prime step. It offers an if or else prediction of an individual based on some health conditions such as BP, blood sugar, troponin and etc; these conditions are passed by an algorithim which predicts about the heart attack which may or may not occour. It also offers a saved stats and last predicted results of that individual which is preserved in the backend of this project. Built with Python, MERN, flask and Sklearn for cross-platform compatibility. An innovative tool to predict any comming heart attack.
